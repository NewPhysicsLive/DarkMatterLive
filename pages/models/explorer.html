Theoretical Model Frameworks

Groupings by spin, field content & coupling structure used for experimental interpretation.

1. Simplified Models

Minimal Lagrangians capturing collider + detection phenomenology.

Spin-0 DM (Scalar $\phi$)

Higgs portal: $\lambda\,\phi^2 |H|^2$.

Spin-1/2 DM (Fermion $\psi$)

Higgs or vector portals; neutralino-like cases.

Spin-1 DM (Vector $V_\mu$)

Kinetic mixing via vector portal.

Effective Field Theory (EFT)

Heavy mediator integrated out ‚Üí higher-dimension operators.

2. Extended Sector Models

Additional fields address DM plus auxiliary puzzles (hierarchy, neutrino mass).

Extended Higgs (2HDM, etc.)

Extra doublets/pseudoscalars; DM couples to second Higgs $a$.

Dark Higgs + Dark Photon

Dark Higgs gives mass to $A'$ enabling multi-mediator signals.

Axion-Like Particles (ALPs)

Generalized axion field $a$ with variable decay constants.

Candidate Mass Spectrum

Illustrative relation of mass scale vs conceptual interaction strength.

WIMPs ($\chi$)

Spin 0/1/2 simplified & extended Higgs.

Axions / ALPs ($a$)

Axion portal & extended sector.

Sterile Neutrinos ($N$)

Neutrino portal; warm DM.
